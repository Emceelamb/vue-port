As a transdisciplinary artist, I experiment with interactive technology and traditional handcraft. My practice seeks to disrupt the notion that there is a place for everything and everything is in its place[1]. I draw on media studies and critical theory to reify art in technology. More simply put: if the Medium is the Message[2], what is the Message and how can I reinterpret it?
